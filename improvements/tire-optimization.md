# Tire Change Speed Optimization

Optimize tire gun torque settings for faster wheel changes.
Target: Reduce tire change time from 2.5s to 2.0s per wheel.

## Changes
- Adjust pneumatic pressure for optimal torque delivery
- Update wheel nut socket specifications  
- Add predictive maintenance for tire guns

## Expected Impact
- 2.0s total pitstop improvement (0.5s per wheel × 4)
- Enhanced crew ergonomics
- Reduced equipment wear
