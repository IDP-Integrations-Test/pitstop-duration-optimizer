# Pre-Stop Tire Warming Strategy

Optimize tire blanket temperatures for immediate grip.

## Temperature Targets
- Soft compound: 95°C (was 90°C)
- Medium compound: 90°C (was 85°C)
- Hard compound: 85°C (was 80°C)

## Benefits
- Immediate grip on pit exit
- Faster out-lap times by 0.3s/lap
- Competitive advantage in traffic

## Testing Requirements
- Thermal degradation analysis
- FIA temperature compliance verification
- Driver feedback integration
