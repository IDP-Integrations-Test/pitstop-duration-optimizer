# Pit Equipment Weight Reduction

Reduce pit equipment weight for faster crew movements.

## Target Components
- Wheel guns: -15% weight (carbon fiber housing)
- Jack systems: -20% weight (titanium structure)
- Fuel hose: -10% weight (advanced composites)

## Impact
- Improved crew endurance
- Faster equipment positioning
- 0.3s average time savings

## Materials
- Carbon fiber composites
- Titanium alloys
- Advanced polymer compounds
